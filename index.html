About Our School

Our school provides training in both modern-day taekwondo and traditional taekwondo. Modern day taekwondo being Kukkiwon which is an Olympic event and traditional taekwondo being Moo Duk Kwan. With experts and teachers and our master together, our school excels in fight training, self-defense, one step sparring, poomsae and meditation.

Why should someone choose taekwondo and what are the benefits?

Taekwondo is a Korean Martial Arts that is characterized by punching and kicking methods, emphasizing on kicking techniques especially. Taekwondo increases awareness, keeping a person calm under pressure. A proper mental state can be achieved through meditation and practice. Taekwondo makes sure that a persons self esteem and self confidence keeps on developing day by day and doesn’t fade away. Fitness level rises with regularity and proper training and one gains a peace in mind.
Self defense is an important aspect to talk about and Taekwondo and Moo Duk Kwan taekwondo will certainly make sure that a person male or female who is practitioner of taekwondo gets the proper self defense training so that they can keep themselves safe and their surroundings safe. Last but certainly not least, Taekwondo provides career opportunities and a path to glory through proper practice and training.
